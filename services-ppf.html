WHAT IS PPF?

Expert PPF Lahore installation at Xtreme Wash

Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a clear, ultra-durable polyurethane layer applied directly over your vehicle's paint. Virtually invisible once installed, PPF acts as a physical barrier against stone chips, scratches, bug splatter, and everyday wear — preserving your factory finish and resale value.

At Xtreme Wash Car Detailing Center, we use premium self-healing PPF films professionally cut and applied for a seamless, bubble-free finish on any vehicle.

COVERAGE OPTIONS

Choose the protection level that fits your driving habits

🚗

FULL BODY PPF

Complete vehicle coverage — hood, bumpers, fenders, doors, mirrors, and more. Maximum protection for new and luxury vehicles.

🛡️

PARTIAL PPF

High-impact zones only — front bumper, hood, fenders, and mirrors. Most popular and cost-effective option for Lahore drivers.

✂️

CUSTOM PPF

Target specific panels, edges, or high-wear areas. Ideal for protecting door cups, rocker panels, or aftermarket parts.

SELF-HEALING TECHNOLOGY

Minor scratches disappear with heat

Premium PPF films feature self-healing top coats. Light swirl marks and scratches from washing or daily use disappear when exposed to warm water, sunlight, or a heat gun. The film's elastomeric polymers flow back to their original shape, keeping your protection layer looking flawless.

This is especially valuable on Lahore's congested roads where close traffic and tight parking create constant minor contact damage.

BUILT FOR LAHORE ROADS

Why PPF is essential in Punjab's driving conditions

Lahore's roads present unique challenges — construction debris on GT Road, gravel on Ring Road, tight parking in commercial areas, and heavy traffic on Mall Road and Ferozepur Road. A single highway trip can pepper your hood with stone chips that cost thousands to repaint.

PPF is the smartest investment for new cars, luxury vehicles, and anyone who wants to maintain factory-fresh paint through Lahore's demanding daily driving environment.
