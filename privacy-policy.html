PRIVACY POLICY

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Home / Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

Xtreme Wash Car Detailing Center ("Xtreme Wash," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website www.xtremewash.pk and provides car detailing services in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, submit our contact form, communicate with us via WhatsApp, or use our services.

By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Contact Form Data

When you submit our contact form, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Vehicle make, model, and year (if provided)
  • Service requested and preferred date/time
  • Location or address (for doorstep service bookings)
  • Any additional message or special instructions you provide

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule appointments, and deliver the services you request.

WhatsApp Communications

When you contact us via WhatsApp (including through links on our website), we may collect:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Messages, photos, and media you send us
  • Vehicle details and service preferences shared in conversation
  • Location information if you share your address or live location for doorstep service

WhatsApp messages are processed through Meta Platforms, Inc. Your use of WhatsApp is also subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy. We retain WhatsApp conversation records only as long as necessary to fulfill service requests and maintain customer records.

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs
  • Date and time of your visit

2. Analytics and Facebook Pixel

We use website analytics tools and the Facebook Pixel (Meta Pixel) to understand how visitors interact with our website and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns. These tools may collect:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Device and browser information
  • Actions taken on our website (such as clicking WhatsApp or call buttons)
  • Conversion events related to our Facebook and Instagram advertising

Data collected through Facebook Pixel is processed by Meta Platforms, Inc. in accordance with Meta's Data Policy. We use this data to improve our website, optimize marketing efforts, and deliver relevant advertisements. You can manage your ad preferences through your Facebook account settings or by using browser-based opt-out tools provided by Meta.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • To schedule, confirm, and deliver car detailing services
  • To send appointment reminders and service updates via phone, email, or WhatsApp
  • To process payments and maintain transaction records
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • To analyze website traffic and marketing campaign performance
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations
  • To protect against fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

Xtreme Wash does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We do not share your personal data with data brokers or unrelated commercial entities.

We may share limited information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our website or business (e.g., hosting providers), under strict confidentiality obligations
  • With Meta Platforms, Inc. through Facebook Pixel and WhatsApp, as described above
  • When required by law, court order, or government authority in Pakistan
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Xtreme Wash, our customers, or others

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytics data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

Types of cookies we may use include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Marketing cookies: Used by Facebook Pixel to track conversions and deliver targeted advertisements

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Contact form submissions and WhatsApp records related to completed services are typically retained for up to three (3) years for customer service and accounting purposes.

7. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Object to certain processing of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days. To request deletion of your data, email us at Xtremewashofficial@Gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include your full name and phone number so we can identify your records.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including Facebook, Instagram, Google Maps, and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

11. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and the Province of Punjab. Any disputes arising from this Privacy Policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
