PAINT CORRECTION LAHORE

Restore your paint to showroom clarity

Paint correction is the process of permanently removing defects from your vehicle's clear coat — swirl marks from improper washing, light scratches, water spots, oxidation, and holograms from cheap polishing. Using machine polishers and professional compounds, our technicians level the clear coat microscopically to reveal true, deep gloss.

Paint correction Lahore is essential before any ceramic or graphene coating — coating over damaged paint locks in imperfections permanently.

CORRECTION STAGES

We recommend the right level based on paint inspection

1️⃣

1-STAGE CORRECTION

Single polish pass removes 50–70% of light swirls. Ideal for well-maintained paint or as maintenance before coating refresh.

2️⃣

2-STAGE CORRECTION

Compound cut followed by finishing polish. Removes 80–90% of defects including moderate swirls and oxidation.

3️⃣

3-STAGE CORRECTION

Heavy compound, medium polish, and finishing glaze for 95%+ defect removal. Required for neglected or black/dark paint.
