GETTING THERE

FROM DHA

Head north on Main Boulevard toward Ghazi Road. Continue onto Ferozepur Road, then turn onto Multan Road. Follow signs toward Singhpura — we're opposite Butt Sweet, near Sodhiwal. Approximate drive: 25–35 minutes depending on traffic.

FROM GULBERG

Take Main Boulevard Gulberg toward Kalma Chowk, then continue on Ferozepur Road. Merge onto Multan Road heading south. Singhpura is on your left near Butt Sweet. Approximate drive: 20–30 minutes.

FROM JOHAR TOWN

Take Abdul Sattar Edhi Road or Khayaban-e-Firdousi toward Multan Road. Continue on Multan Road — Singhpura is a short drive south. Look for Butt Sweet as your landmark. Approximate drive: 15–25 minutes.

Prefer not to drive? Book our doorstep service and we'll come to your home or office anywhere in Lahore.
