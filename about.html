OUR STORY

From a passion for perfection to Lahore's go-to detailing center

Xtreme Wash Car Detailing Center was founded with a simple mission: bring world-class car care to every vehicle owner in Lahore. What started as a commitment to delivering showroom-quality results has grown into one of the city's most trusted auto detailing facilities, proudly serving customers from Singhpura and across DHA, Gulberg, Johar Town, and beyond.

Located in the heart of Singhpura — opposite Butt Sweet, near Sodhiwal — our center combines international-grade products, professional equipment, and certified technicians to transform every car we touch. Whether you need a quick wash, full premium detailing, graphene coating, PPF installation, or paint correction, we treat every vehicle with the same Xtreme standard of excellence.

With 24/7 availability and convenient doorstep service, we've made premium car care accessible to busy Lahore residents who refuse to compromise on quality. Our reputation is built on transparency, craftsmanship, and results that speak for themselves.
