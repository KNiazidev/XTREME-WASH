GRAPHENE COATING LAHORE

Next-generation paint protection for Lahore's harsh conditions

Graphene coating represents the pinnacle of automotive paint protection. At Xtreme Wash, we apply premium graphene formulas rated at 10H hardness — the highest on the Mohs scale for coatings — delivering up to 7 years of protection with incredible super reflection and mirror-like depth.

Unlike traditional wax that lasts weeks, graphene forms a permanent nano-layer that repels water, resists UV fading, fights Lahore's dust and pollution, and keeps your paint looking wet-gloss fresh year after year.

CERAMIC COATING OPTION

Quality protection at a more accessible price point

Not ready for graphene? Our professional ceramic coating Lahore service still delivers excellent 9H hardness protection with 2–5 year durability. Ideal for daily drivers who want significant paint protection without the premium graphene investment. Every ceramic job includes full paint decontamination and preparation.
